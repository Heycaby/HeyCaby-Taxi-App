# Driver Sound Pack Guide

Put Driver app sounds here:

- `assets/sounds/driver/`
- `assets/sounds/shared/`

Use lowercase filenames with underscores, for example:

- `ride_request_incoming.mp3`
- `driver_cancelled.mp3`
- `general_notification.mp3`
- `trip_complete.mp3`

Full detailed naming plan is documented in:

- `apps/rider/assets/sounds/SOUND_GUIDE.md`
