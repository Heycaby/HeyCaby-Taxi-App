# Privacy Policy — Driver App (Summary)

**Effective date: 1 May 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how HeyCaby processes your personal data when you use the platform as a driver.

---

## 1. Data we collect

HeyCaby may process the following categories of personal data:

• **Account data** — such as your name, email address, and phone number
• **Driver profile data** — including service area, pricing, and availability
• **Vehicle data** — including license plate, vehicle type, and registration details
• **Compliance data** — including licences, permits, insurance documents, and verification status
• **Trip and earnings data** — including ride requests, trip activity, and earnings records
• **Support data** — including messages and support requests
• **Technical data** — including device information, app usage, and security logs

**Legal basis:**
We process this data based on performance of contract (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR), legal obligations (Article 6(1)(c)), and legitimate interests such as platform security and fraud prevention (Article 6(1)(f)).

---

## 2. Location data

Your location is processed only when necessary for driver operations, including when you are online, receiving ride requests, or actively completing a trip.

Location data is used for:

• Matching riders and drivers
• Navigation context
• Safety and fraud prevention
• Service quality improvements

When you are offline, continuous live location tracking is not active.

**Legal basis:** performance of contract and legitimate interest.

---

## 3. Why we process data

We process personal data to:

• Operate and maintain the platform
• Match drivers with riders
• Maintain payment records and invoices
• Verify legal compliance and driver eligibility
• Provide customer support
• Prevent fraud and abuse
• Improve platform performance and safety

---

## 4. AI support (Lee)

HeyCaby offers an optional AI support assistant called Lee.

If you choose to use AI chat:

• Your messages and limited support context are processed by third-party AI providers (including OpenAI)
• AI processing is only activated after clear notice and your explicit consent
• You should not share sensitive personal or financial data in AI chat

You can always choose to use human support instead.

**Legal basis:** explicit consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR).

---

## 5. Data sharing

HeyCaby does not sell your personal data.

We may share data only where necessary with:

• Service providers (such as hosting, payments, and verification providers)
• Legal authorities where required by law
• Partners strictly required to operate the platform

All third parties are bound by data processing agreements and GDPR obligations.

---

## 6. Veriff verification data (third-party)

Identity and licence verification is performed by Veriff as an independent data processor.

Veriff processes and stores verification data within its own secure systems.

HeyCaby only receives and stores limited verification metadata necessary to confirm compliance status.

HeyCaby does not store full identity document copies within the app database.

Where required by law, verification data may be shared with competent authorities.

---

## 7. Retention and security

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for:

• Operational purposes
• Legal and tax obligations
• Dispute handling and fraud prevention
• Safety and compliance

We apply appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

---

## 8. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

• Access your personal data
• Correct inaccurate data
• Request deletion of your data
• Restrict or object to processing
• Request data portability

You can exercise your rights via in-app support or by contacting us directly.
