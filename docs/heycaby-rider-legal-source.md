## Privacy Policy - HeyCaby

Last updated: [Add Date]

HeyCaby ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our platform.

### 1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide our services:

- Account Information: email address and basic profile details for account creation and identity verification
- Location Data: used only during active bookings to match riders with nearby drivers and facilitate trips
- Trip Data: pickup/drop-off locations, timestamps, and ride history for receipts and service improvement
- Device Data: app version, device type, and push notification tokens for functionality and performance

### 2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used strictly to operate the HeyCaby platform:

- Match riders with available drivers
- Facilitate ride bookings and communication
- Provide trip history and receipts
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Send important notifications (e.g. ride updates, account alerts)

We do NOT use your data for advertising or sell your data to third parties.

### 3. Location Data Usage

- Location is only accessed during active ride sessions
- We do not track users in the background outside of bookings
- Location data is not stored longer than necessary for trip completion

### 4. Data Sharing

We share limited data only when required to provide the service:

- Drivers receive: rider name (or alias) and pickup location
- Riders receive: driver details necessary for the trip

We do NOT share:

- Email addresses
- Phone numbers (unless explicitly required in future features)
- Sensitive personal data

### 5. Data Retention

- Trip data is stored for receipts and history
- Account data is stored until account deletion is requested
- Temporary data (like recent searches) may expire automatically

### 6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As a user in the EU, you have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your account
- Restrict or object to data processing

You can delete your account directly in the app:
Account -> Delete Account

### 7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure, and we encourage users to keep their account information safe.

### 8. Push Notifications

We may send notifications related to:

- Ride updates
- Important service messages
- Occasional product updates

You can disable notifications in your device settings at any time.

### 9. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted services such as:

- Payment providers (e.g., Mollie, Tikkie)
- Infrastructure services (e.g., Firebase, Supabase)

These providers process data only as necessary to deliver their services.

### 10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app means you accept the updated policy.

### 11. Contact

For privacy-related requests:
Contact us via the app support section.

## Terms of Service - HeyCaby

Last updated: [Add Date]

### 1. About HeyCaby

HeyCaby is a platform that connects riders with independent, licensed taxi drivers. We do not employ drivers and do not provide transportation services ourselves. We act solely as a matching and facilitation platform.

### 2. User Roles

There are two types of users:

- Riders: individuals requesting transportation
- Drivers: independent professionals providing transportation

Each user is responsible for their own actions on the platform.

### 3. Rider Responsibilities

As a rider, you agree to:

- Provide accurate pickup and destination information
- Be present at the pickup location on time
- Treat drivers with respect and professionalism
- Pay for completed rides using agreed methods
- Not engage in illegal, abusive, or unsafe behavior

Failure to meet these responsibilities may result in account suspension.

### 4. Driver Responsibilities

Drivers using HeyCaby must:

- Hold valid licenses and permits required by law
- Provide safe, lawful, and professional transport services
- Arrive at pickup locations on time
- Communicate clearly with riders
- Set fair and transparent pricing
- Handle payments directly with riders

Drivers are fully responsible for their services, conduct, and compliance with local regulations.

### 5. Payments

- Payments are made directly between rider and driver
- HeyCaby does not process, hold, or guarantee payments
- Available payment methods may include cash, card (PIN), or third-party apps (e.g. Tikkie)

Any disputes regarding payment must be resolved between rider and driver.

### 6. Cancellations

- Riders may cancel before driver acceptance at no cost
- After acceptance, cancellation fees may apply at the driver’s discretion
- Repeated cancellations or no-shows may result in account restrictions

### 7. Platform Usage

You agree NOT to:

- Misuse the platform
- Provide false information
- Attempt fraud or payment abuse
- Harass or harm other users

We reserve the right to suspend or remove accounts that violate these terms.

### 8. Account Suspension

HeyCaby may suspend or terminate accounts in cases of:

- Fraudulent activity
- Abuse or harassment
- Repeated no-shows or cancellations
- Violations of these terms

### 9. Disputes

- Users should first resolve disputes directly
- If needed, disputes can be reported through the app
- HeyCaby may assist but is not liable for outcomes between users

### 10. Liability

HeyCaby is not liable for:

- The actions of drivers or riders
- The quality or safety of rides
- Any damages, losses, or disputes arising from trips

Users accept that HeyCaby is a facilitator, not a transport provider.

### 11. Changes to Terms

We may update these terms at any time. Continued use of the platform means you accept the updated terms.

### 12. Contact

For support or disputes:
Use the in-app support feature.
